Islamabad High Court

  About High Court

Message of the Honourable Chief Justice:

Dispensation of Justice, strictly in accordance with the law is the sole concept any judicial system should adhere to. Islamabad High Court, as the youngest High Court of the country, has come a long way in achieving this goal. In the short span of time since its inception, it has achieved significant milestones for enforcement of fundamental rights and adjudication of public and private litigation, expeditiously. The Institution shall continue to endeavor to administer speedy justice to everyone, without fear or favour, in accordance with the law.

History of the High Court:

Located in Pothohar Plateau, the region has historically been a part of the cross roads of Punjab and Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, with Margalla pass acting as a gateway between the two regions. The city was build during 1960’s to replace Karachi as Pakistan’s Capital. Historically the territory is regarded to be one of the earliest sites of human civilization in Asia.
